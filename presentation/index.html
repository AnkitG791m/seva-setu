📌 The Problem

Crisis Response is Broken

⏳

Delayed Triage

Authorities manually analyze reports, wasting the critical "Golden Hour" while situations worsen.

📍

No Location Intelligence

Volunteers are deployed arbitrarily — without knowing which zones are most critically affected.

🗂️

Resource Wastage

Duplicate efforts in some areas while high-urgency needs in others go ignored completely.

"During the 2023 Flood Response in Assam, 40% of NGO resources were misdirected due to lack of a central prioritization system — costing 72+ hours of critical response time."

💡 Our Solution

SevaSetu: End-to-End AI Coordination

🤖

AI-Driven Urgency Scoring

Google Gemini 1.5 Flash analyzes every report and assigns an urgency score (0–100) in real-time — medical, water, food, shelter — prioritized instantly.

🗺️

Geospatial Crisis Heatmap

Google Maps displays a real-time heatmap of active need reports. Coordinators see exactly where to deploy volunteers for maximum impact.

📸

Citizen-Powered Reporting

Community members submit geo-tagged reports with photos (stored in GCS) via a simple mobile-friendly UI with Places Autocomplete.

🔔

Instant Volunteer Alerts

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) pushes real-time task assignments to volunteers the moment a high-urgency need is identified nearby.
